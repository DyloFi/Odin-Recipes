Vegemite on Toast

Description

Delicious Vegemite On Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread (Better if its Wholegrain.)
  • Vegemite
  • Buttter
  • Milk (Dont even bother if you dont have the Milk.)

Steps

  1. Put bread in Toaster.
  2. Go have a Duz while you wait for that ish to toast.
  3. When bread is toasted, spread on some delicious butter relativly quickly so it melts. (You dont want that unmelted shit on your toast.)
  4. Spread a light amount of Vegemite on top of that.
  5. Pour a glass of Milk.
  6. Get ya Milk Moustache on.
